This chilaquiles recipe is origined in Guatemala as an alternative to the Mexican chilaquiles

  • Tortillas
  • Onion & Tomatoes
  • Meat
  • Guacamole
  • Cream
  • Lemons
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Chop the onions and tomatoes
  2. Grill some tortillas with mozzarella cheese and cut them on nachos pieces
  3. Slice some lettuce
  4. Put the sliced tortillas on the plate and start putting the lettuce
  5. Finish putting the cheese and cream above, decorate at your preference
  6. Enjoy your Chilaquiles!
