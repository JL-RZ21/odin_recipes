This birria tacos recipe is origined in Guatemala as an alternative to the Mexican birria tacos

  • Birria meat
  • Onion
  • Mozzarela cheese
  • Tortillas
  • Tomatoes
  • Lemons

Steps:

  1. Chop the onions and tomatoes
  2. Grill some tortillas with mozzarella cheese
  3. Slice some lemons for the juice
  4. Put the Birria meat on the grilled tortillas and warm them a little
  5. Build your taco as you like with the lemon juice and chopped onions and tomatoes
